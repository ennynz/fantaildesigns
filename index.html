45 years of experience
tailoring to fit like a glove

Fantail Designs was established over 45 years ago in Nelson, New Zealand.
After working in Auckland, Los Angeles and now San Diego,
owner Faith Bartosh-Medina has decades of experience in
dressmaking, pattern making, clothing design and interior design.
She has a stunning team of qualified staff to provide the best alteration,
design and dressmaking services for you. With superior service and
attention to detail, Fantail designs is who you can count on. Tell us
what you want and we will create one to one service just for you.
And don’t forget all the details, the bits and pieces; we are a one stop shop
to make your life easier.
